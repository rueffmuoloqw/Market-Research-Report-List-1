<p><strong>The Trimellitic Anhydride Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MC Method</li><li>MGC Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Trimellitic Anhydride (TMA) market can be categorized based on the production methods: the MC (Methyl Cyclopentadiene) Method and the MGC (Methyl Glycolic Acid) Method. The MC Method involves cyclization of maleic anhydride with methyl cyclopentadiene, yielding TMA with specific properties beneficial for resin and coating applications. In contrast, the MGC Method utilizes methyl glycolic acid for production, which typically results in different performance characteristics and applications, leading to varied demand across industries such as adhesives and plastics.</p></p>
