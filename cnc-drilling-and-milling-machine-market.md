<p><strong>The CNC Drilling and Milling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CNC drilling and milling machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their spindle orientation. Vertical CNC machines have a spindle that operates perpendicular to the workpiece, making them ideal for tasks requiring precision and intricate cuts, often used in small to medium-sized components. In contrast, horizontal CNC machines feature a spindle that runs parallel to the workpiece, which is better suited for heavy-duty tasks and larger components, allowing for efficient chip removal and enhanced stability during machining processes.</p></p>
