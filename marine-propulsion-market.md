<p><p>The marine propulsion market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. Renewable: Incorporates clean energy sources like wind or solar to minimize environmental impact.  </p><p>4. Nuclear: Offers high efficiency with minimal fuel usage, mainly for large vessels.  </p><p>5. Gas Turbine: Delivers high power-to-weight ratios, ideal for fast vessels.  </p><p>6. Fuel Cell: Converts hydrogen to electricity for clean propulsion.  </p><p>7. Hybrid: Combines traditional and renewable sources for flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
