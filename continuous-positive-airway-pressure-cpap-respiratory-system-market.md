<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) respiratory system market consists of two primary types: fixed pressure CPAP devices and auto-adjusting CPAP devices. Fixed pressure CPAP devices deliver a consistent, predetermined pressure throughout the night, providing stable airflow to keep air passages open. In contrast, auto-adjusting CPAP devices dynamically adjust the pressure based on real-time airflow needs, offering personalized treatment for varying levels of obstructive sleep apnea. Both types aim to improve patient comfort and efficacy in respiratory support.</p></p>
