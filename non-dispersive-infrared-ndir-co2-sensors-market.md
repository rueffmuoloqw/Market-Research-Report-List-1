<p><p>Non-dispersive infrared (NDIR) CO2 sensors are classified into single-channel and dual-channel types based on their design and functionality. Single-channel NDIR sensors measure CO2 levels using one infrared light path, providing a cost-effective solution for basic applications. In contrast, dual-channel NDIR sensors incorporate two light paths, enhancing accuracy and sensitivity by compensating for factors like temperature and humidity. This results in improved performance in demanding environments, making dual-channel sensors preferred for applications requiring precise CO2 monitoring.</p></p>
