<p><strong>The Steam Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roots-Type</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam compressors market is primarily categorized into two types: Roots-type and centrifugal compressors. Roots-type compressors utilize a pair of meshing lobes to compress steam, delivering consistent, low-pressure performance suitable for various industrial applications. Conversely, centrifugal compressors utilize high-speed rotating impellers to increase steam pressure, offering high efficiency and capacity for larger systems. Each type serves distinct demands, with Roots-type ideal for lower volume needs and centrifugal compressors favored for high-volume, high-pressure applications, catering to diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
