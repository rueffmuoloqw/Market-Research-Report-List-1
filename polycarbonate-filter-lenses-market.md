<p><p>The polycarbonate filter lenses market is categorized into three types: weak filters, neutral filters, and strong filters. Weak filters offer minimal light attenuation, suitable for enhancing color perception without significant distortion. Neutral filters provide a balanced light reduction, maintaining color fidelity while reducing brightness for improved visibility. Strong filters significantly diminish light transmission, ideal for bright environments or specific applications like photography. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to diverse consumer preferences in eyewear and protective gear.</p></p>
