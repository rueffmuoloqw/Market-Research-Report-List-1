<p><p>The Residual Stress Measurement Service Market encompasses two primary types: macro stresses and micro stresses. Macro stresses refer to the large-scale residual stresses present in materials and structures, often influenced by manufacturing processes, temperature changes, or external loading. Micro stresses, on the other hand, pertain to residual stresses at a microscopic level, which can arise from processes like welding or casting. Both types are critical for assessing material performance, durability, and failure risks in various industrial applications.</p></p>
