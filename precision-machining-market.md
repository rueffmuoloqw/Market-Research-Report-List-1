<p><strong>The Precision Machining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Milling</li><li>Turning</li><li>Electrical Discharge Machining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precision machining market comprises various methods, including milling, turning, and electrical discharge machining (EDM). Milling involves removing material from a workpiece using rotary cutters, ideal for producing intricate shapes and surfaces. Turning, on the other hand, involves rotating a workpiece against a cutting tool to create cylindrical parts, known for its efficiency in producing consistent dimensions. EDM employs electrical sparks to erode material from a workpiece, suitable for creating complex shapes and work with hard materials, offering high precision and finish.</p></p>
