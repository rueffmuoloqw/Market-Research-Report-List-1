<p><p>C-mounted filters are essential optical components used in various applications, including industrial imaging and telecommunications. The market is primarily segmented into bandpass filters and longpass filters. Bandpass filters allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass while blocking others, making them ideal for applications requiring precise spectral separation. Longpass filters, on the other hand, permit longer wavelengths to pass while blocking shorter ones, commonly used in fluorescence imaging and other optical systems. Both types enhance image quality and signal clarity in their respective applications.</p></p>
