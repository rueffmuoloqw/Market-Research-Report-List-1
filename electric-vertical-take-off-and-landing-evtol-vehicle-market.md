<p><p>The Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing (eVTOL) vehicle market encompasses several types, including vector thrust, cruising, and multi-rotor designs. Vector thrust vehicles utilize tilting rotors or jets for versatile maneuverability during take-off, landing, and flight. Cruising eVTOLs focus on efficient, sustained flight, often designed for longer distances. Multi-rotor vehicles use multiple vertical rotors to achieve stability and lift, making them ideal for short-range or urban air mobility applications. Each type offers unique advantages for specific use cases.</p></p>
