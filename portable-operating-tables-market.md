<p><strong>The Portable Operating Tables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electro-hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable operating tables can be classified into four main types: manual, electric, hydraulic, and electro-hydraulic. Manual tables require physical effort for adjustments and positioning, making them cost-effective but less convenient. Electric tables offer motorized adjustments for height and tilt, enhancing ease of use. Hydraulic tables utilize hydraulic systems for smooth adjustments, providing stability and support during procedures. Electro-hydraulic tables combine both electric and hydraulic features, allowing for precise control and versatility, catering to diverse surgical needs and environments.</p></p>
