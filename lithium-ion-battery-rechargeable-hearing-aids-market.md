<p><p>The rechargeable hearing aids market features various types, including Behind-the-Ear (BTE), Receiver-in-Canal (RIC), In-The-Ear (ITE), and In-The-Canal (ITC). BTE models sit behind the ear, offering durability and battery life. RIC aids are discreet with the receiver located in the ear canal, providing natural sound quality. ITE devices fill the outer ear for a custom fit, while ITC aids are smaller, fitting partially in the canal for a low-profile solution. Lithium-ion batteries enhance convenience with quick recharging capabilities.</p></p>
