<p><p>Ultrasonic liposuction devices, specifically ultrasonic assisted liposuction (UAL), utilize ultrasound energy to emulsify fat cells, enhancing fat removal efficiency. The market is categorized into two types: internal UAL and external UAL. Internal UAL involves inserting a specialized cannula into the body, delivering ultrasound waves directly to the targeted fat, providing precision. In contrast, external UAL employs ultrasound technology externally on the skin surface, suitable for non-invasive fat reduction methods, catering to varying patient needs and desired outcomes.</p></p>
