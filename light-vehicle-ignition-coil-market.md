<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle ignition coil market is divided into single-spark and multi-spark types. Single-spark ignition coils generate one high-voltage spark per engine cycle, ensuring reliable ignition in conventional engines. In contrast, multi-spark ignition coils produce multiple sparks during a single cycle, optimizing combustion efficiency and improving performance in modern engines. Multi-spark systems enhance engine response, fuel economy, and reduce emissions, making them increasingly popular as automotive technology advances and emissions standards become more stringent.</p></p>
