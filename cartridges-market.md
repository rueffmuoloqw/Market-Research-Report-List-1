<p><strong>The Cartridges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split Cartridge</li><li>Integrated Cartridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cartridges market mainly consists of two types: Split Cartridge and Integrated Cartridge. Split Cartridges feature separate components for the ink and print head, allowing users to replace either part independently, which can reduce costs and waste. In contrast, Integrated Cartridges combine both ink and print head in a single unit, simplifying installation and maintenance but typically resulting in higher replacement costs. Each type serves different consumer needs, balancing convenience, cost-effectiveness, and environmental considerations in printer usage.</p></p>
