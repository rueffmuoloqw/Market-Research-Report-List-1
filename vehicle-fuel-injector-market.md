<p><ul><li>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI)</li><li>Port Fuel Injectors (PFI)</li><li>Diesel Fuel Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle fuel injector market encompasses various types, primarily including Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), Port Fuel Injectors (PFI), and Diesel Fuel Injectors. GDI technology injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency and power. PFI injectors, on the other hand, deliver fuel into the intake manifold, ensuring good mixing with air for combustion. Diesel fuel injectors operate at higher pressures, atomizing fuel for efficient combustion in diesel engines. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing engine performance and emissions.</p></p>
