<p><p>The Spark Machining market encompasses three main types: Sinker EDM, Wire EDM, and Hole Drilling EDM. Sinker EDM utilizes electrode shapes to create intricate cavities in conductive materials, ideal for complex parts. Wire EDM employs a thin, electrically charged wire to cut through metal with extreme precision, suitable for flat and contoured shapes. Hole Drilling EDM focuses on creating precise holes in hard materials, often for aerospace and manufacturing applications. Each type addresses specific machining needs, ensuring versatility in industrial processes.</p></p>
