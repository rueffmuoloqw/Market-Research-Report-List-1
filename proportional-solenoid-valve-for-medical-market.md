<p><p>Proportional solenoid valves for the medical market are essential for precise fluid control in medical devices. Direct acting proportional solenoid valves offer high responsiveness and can directly control flow rates, making them suitable for applications requiring immediate feedback. In contrast, pilot proportional solenoid valves utilize a smaller pilot valve to control larger flow rates, providing greater efficiency and reduced power consumption. Both types are crucial for ensuring accurate dosing, pressure regulation, and maintaining optimal performance in medical instrumentation.</p></p>
