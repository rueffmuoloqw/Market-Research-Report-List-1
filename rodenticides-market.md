<p><strong>The Rodenticides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anticoagulants</li><li>Non-Anticoagulants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rodenticides market is divided into two main types: anticoagulants and non-anticoagulants. Anticoagulants work by disrupting the blood clotting process in rodents, leading to death after multiple feedings. They are often preferred for their effectiveness and low risk of secondary poisoning. Non-anticoagulants, on the other hand, act quickly and usually require a single feeding, targeting the nervous system or causing dehydration. Both types are used in various settings, including agriculture, urban areas, and food storage facilities.</p></p>
