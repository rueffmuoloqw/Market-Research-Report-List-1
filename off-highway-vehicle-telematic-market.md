<p><p>The Off-Highway Vehicle Telematics market is broadly categorized into two primary types: cellular and satellite. Cellular telematics utilizes mobile networks to transmit data, offering widespread coverage and lower costs, making it suitable for areas with reliable cellular service. In contrast, satellite telematics provides global coverage, making it ideal for remote or rural locations where cellular signals may be weak or nonexistent. Each technology caters to specific operational needs and environmental conditions, enhancing vehicle tracking, monitoring, and management.</p></p>
