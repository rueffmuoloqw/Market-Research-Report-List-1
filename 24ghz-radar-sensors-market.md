<p><p>The 24GHz radar sensors market is segmented into two main types: microwave and millimeter wave. Microwave radar operates typically in the frequency range of 1 to 30 GHz, offering robust performance for various applications, including automotive and industrial uses. Millimeter wave radar, part of the higher-frequency spectrum (30 GHz to 300 GHz), provides enhanced resolution and accuracy, making it ideal for applications like advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and surveillance.
