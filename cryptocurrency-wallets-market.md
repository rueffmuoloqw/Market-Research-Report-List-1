<p><p>Cryptocurrency wallets can be classified into four main market types. Hardware wallets are physical devices that store private keys offline, providing high security against hacks. Software wallets are applications installed on computers or smartphones, offering convenience for transactions. Online wallets, or web-based wallets, allow users to manage their cryptocurrencies through the internet, offering easy accessibility but potentially lower security.</p></p>
