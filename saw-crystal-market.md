<p><p>The SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) crystal market primarily comprises three types: Quartz, Lithium Tantalate (LiTaO3), and Lithium Niobate (LiNbO3). Quartz crystals are widely used for their stable frequency characteristics in communication devices. LiTaO3 is favored for its excellent electro-optic properties, making it ideal for high-frequency applications and sensors. LiNbO3, known for its superior non-linear optical properties, is utilized in advanced telecommunications and signal processing. Each type excels in specific applications, driving demand across various technological sectors.</p></p>
