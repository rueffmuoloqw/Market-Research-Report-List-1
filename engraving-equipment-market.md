<p><p>The engraving equipment market is primarily segmented into laser engraving and mechanical engraving technologies. Laser engraving employs focused light beams to precisely etch designs onto various materials, offering high speed and intricate detail. In contrast, mechanical engraving utilizes physical tools, such as rotary bits or anvils, to cut or engrave materials, providing a tactile approach suitable for robust applications. Both methods cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, crafting, and personalization, each with distinct advantages depending on the desired outcome.</p></p>
